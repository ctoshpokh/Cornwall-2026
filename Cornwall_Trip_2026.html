Must See
🌊 Kynance Cove + Wavecrest Café
45 mins · Lizard Peninsula

One of the most stunning beaches in Cornwall. Turquoise water, white sand, dramatic rocks. Best at low tide. Wavecrest Café sits right on the cliffs at Lizard Point.

💡 Add onto Day 3 on the way back from St Michael's Mount
Must See
🏰 Tintagel Castle
1h 10 mins · North Cornwall

Legendary clifftop castle — one of the most photogenic spots in Cornwall. Jaw-dropping coastal views. Book ahead, arrive at 10am to beat queues.

💡 Best as a Day 4 trip with Boscastle & Port Isaac
Must See
⚓ Boscastle Harbour
10 mins from Tintagel

Dramatic natural harbour carved into the cliffs. Atmospheric and beautiful — great photos. Easy add-on to the Tintagel day.

Must See
🎣 Port Isaac
20 mins from Tintagel

Tiny, impossibly pretty fishing village — the setting for Doc Martin. Cobbled lanes, a pretty harbour, brilliant fish & chips.

Nice to Visit
🏄 Fistral Beach, Newquay
45 mins · North Cornwall

Cornwall's famous surf beach. Great if anyone fancies a lesson — schools right on the sand.

Nice to Visit
🌊 Towan Beach, Newquay
Next to Fistral

More sheltered than Fistral. Famous for the private island house connected by a swing bridge.
